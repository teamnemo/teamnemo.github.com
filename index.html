MyClab

This is where you shall suffer as you take all your quizzes!

Plan

To design and implement MyClab, an educational web-based application that will include a collection of multiple-choice questions categorized by complexity. Students will be able to log-in to the website and take quizzes to evaluate their current knowledge of concepts taught in class. Student will be able to make various attempts on any quiz. In addition, quizzes will be separated into various templates which will be selected randomly so the students will have a variety of questions for the same difficulty level. The program will keep track of both individual and collective scores for each quiz taken, thus allowing the instructor to analyze the needs of the students in the class.
